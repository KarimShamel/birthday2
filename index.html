A Letter & A Promise

💌

June 15, 2026

My dearest Nour,

One year ago today, I stood across from you and made the easiest promise of my life. I promised to be yours — fully, completely, without reservation — and every single day since has only deepened that certainty.

This year has held so much: mornings that were too quiet, evenings that were too loud, moments that made us both cry and moments that made us collapse laughing. Through all of it, you were the constant I kept reaching for. You are my soft landing and my greatest adventure — all at once.

I love the life we are building together, stone by stone, laugh by laugh, bowl of koshary by bowl of koshary. I love who I am when I am next to you. I love that you challenge me, believe in me, and still choose me on the hard days.

Here is to a lifetime more of choosing each other.

Yours — always and completely,
Omar (عمر)
